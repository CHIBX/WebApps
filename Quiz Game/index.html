QUIZ GAME

Presenting to You The New Quiz Game

Many of you have seen the popular show, Who Wants To Be A Millionaire! , and the amount of cash prize presented to whoever can pass the checkpoints set. Well, sorry to say, there won't be any cash prize in this one, but you will be crowned as Albert Einstein's apprentice.

The quiz consists of features similar to those of Who Wants To Be A Millionaire! i.e a counter with levels and utility helpers.

** You get the chance to pick number of questions you want, with various choices of question types including hints and powerups **

Note: Some features are unavailable due to app being rendered while in production. This is only a test as things could go wrong!
